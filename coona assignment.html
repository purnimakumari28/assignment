COVID-19

Coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19) is a contagious disease that caused by the severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 (SARS-CoV-2).The first known case was identified in WUHAN,CHINA,in December 2019. The disease has since spread worldwide,leading to an ongoing pandemic.

The first case of COVID-19 in India were reported on 30 January 2020in three towns of KERALA, among three Indian medical students who had returned from Wuhan,the epicenter of the pandemic.

SYMPTOMS

Most common symptoms are:-

  • fever
  • cough
  • tiredness
  • loss of taste or smell

Serious symptoms:-

  • chest pain
  • shortness of breath

TREATMENTS

Self-care

  • Isolate yourself in a well ventilated room.
  • Follow respiratory etiquettes at all times.
  • Monitor oxygen saturation with a pulse oximeter daily.

Medical treatments

  • Patients may perform warm water gargles or take steam inhakation twice a day.
  • Patients to follow symptomatic management for fever, running nose and cough,as warranted.

PREVENTIONS

To prevent the spread of COVID-19

  • Stay home if you feel unwell.
  • Clean your hands often.Use soap and water ,or an alcohol-based hand rub.
  • Maintain a safe distance from others(at least 1 meter),even if they don't appear to be sick.
